Gmail Setup Instructions
  1. Go to your Google Account settings at myaccount.google.com
  2. Navigate to Security and enable 2-Step Verification if not already enabled
  3. Go to App passwords (search for it in the search bar)
  4. Select "Mail" as the app and "Other" as the device, then enter "CTE Timeclock"
  5. Copy the 16-character password and paste it into the SMTP Password field above
  6. Enter your Gmail address in both Username and From Email fields
